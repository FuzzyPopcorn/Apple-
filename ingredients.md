# **INGREDIENTS**
## Crust
2 ½ cups flour

1 teaspoon salt

1 ½ sticks butter, 1 1/2 sticks, cold, cubed

8 tablespoons ice water, or as needed

## Filling
2 ½ lb granny smith apple, cored, sliced, peeled

¾ cup sugar

2 tablespoons flour

½ teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon cinnamon

¼ teaspoon nutmeg

½ lemon

1 egg, beaten

1 tablespoon sugar
